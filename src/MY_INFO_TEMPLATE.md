## üí° Tips for Writing Good Content

### Bio
- ‚úÖ Be authentic and personal
- ‚úÖ Show enthusiasm for what you do
- ‚úÖ Mention what makes you unique
- ‚ùå Don't be overly formal
- ‚ùå Don't use buzzwords without substance

### Projects
- ‚úÖ Lead with the problem you solved
- ‚úÖ Include specific technologies
- ‚úÖ Mention measurable results if possible
- ‚úÖ Explain what you learned
- ‚ùå Don't just list features

### Experience
- ‚úÖ Start with action verbs (Built, Developed, Designed)
- ‚úÖ Quantify impact when possible
- ‚úÖ Be specific about technologies
- ‚ùå Don't copy-paste job descriptions

### Skills
- ‚úÖ Only list skills you can discuss in an interview
- ‚úÖ Organize by category
- ‚úÖ Keep updated
- ‚ùå Don't exaggerate your proficiency
